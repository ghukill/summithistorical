The Summit Historical Society is dedicated to the discovery, preservation, interpretation, and exhibition of the rich heritage and history of Summit County. We provide tours of historic buildings and mine sites, collections of artifacts, photographs, and documents, and public education programs. Whether your interests lie in our gold rush days, skiing history, or our Native American heritage, come visit us at the Summit. This website also intends to bring together and keep informed all members and friends of the Summit Historical Society. Here you will find here such things as historical sites, Society events, tours, archives and research, Society newsletters, books for sale, and Society Book Club events. Also information on volunteering with the Society and membership.

About Us

The Summit Historical Society (SHS) was incorporated on October 25, 1966, as a private, nonprofit, tax-exempt State of Colorado 501(c) (3) corporation. As such, contributions are tax deductible as permitted by section 170 IRS Code.
